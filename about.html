Our Story

Hey, ya'll! I'm Brittany, the owner of Make it Rain Bows.
My love for bows started when I had my first daughter in 2019
and soon became obsessed with bows, headwraps, top knots, etc.
She gathered a massive collection and wore them every day. Soon
after her second birthday, I had my second daughter. I had a
hard time finding bows that would stand on such a small baby,
and bought some secondhand fabric to try my hand at making
them myself. I found a love for bow making and creating things
for children with my hands. Thus, Make it Rain Bows was born.
Our bows are made with soft, stretchy bullet-liverpool fabric
for newborns to adults. Thank you for shopping small and
don't hesistate to reach out with questions about products,
wholesale inquiries, and even just to chat.
